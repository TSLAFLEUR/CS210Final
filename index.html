About Me:

Hello, my name is Tyler LaFleur. I was born and raised in San Diego, California where I started to gain an interest in computers. Also, thanks to my background in the Marine Corps, I am also skilled in being able to not only listen and follow directions from clients, but I am also able to lead teams efforts. This allows me to be able to work effectively with others while being able to communicate efficiently.
